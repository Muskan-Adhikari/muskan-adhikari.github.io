Overview

New Arunodaya English Secondary School was founded in 1992 A.D. (2048 B.S.) as a Private educational institution, which is located in Dallu, Kathmandu, Nepal. It is affiliated to the National Examination Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education. It offers educational Programs from Playgroup to Grade 10 and  Plus Two programs under Management streams.

New Arunodaya English Higher Secondary School is a co-educational institute fully devoted to the development of students’ capabilities through quality education along with various co-curricular and extra-curricular activities. It has been teaching numerous students at +2 level in management stream with the sole purpose of producing the competent, capable and devoted manpower the nation needs for its all-round development, it was founded to provide quality education to the middle and lower class students at a reasonable and affordable fee which this institution has been carrying out with full sincerity and sense of responsibility since its establishment.

This institute aims at imparting quality education to today's students at a reasonable affordable fee so as to male them able to know themselves and cope with the challenges of the modern world with the full sense of duty towards their own society and nation as well.

New Arunodaya English Secondary School provides up to Secondary level education with moderate fee structures and also provides a scholarship scheme for deserving students.

Salient Features: Cafeteria, Library,  Sports, Counselling, Classrooms, Parking, Conference, Tour, Music, Dance, ECA,  etc.
